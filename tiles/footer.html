JERICO TENNIS CLUB

For over 100 years, Jericho Tennis Club has built an esteemed reputation as one of Vancouver’s most exclusive, yet welcoming private clubs. From world class tennis and squash to athletics and an unmatched social scene, membership is about being a part of a unique, oceanside community on the spectacular shores of Jericho Beach.
